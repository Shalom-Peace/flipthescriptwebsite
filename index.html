What we believe

"Every good story is about transformation. And Jesus is our transformation."

Flip The Script is a Christ-centered collective on a mission to flip everyday moments — movies, music, dramas, novels — into windows that reveal God's redemptive love. We believe He's in all of it. And we gather, reflect, and intercede because of it.

How we live this out

Christ-centered commentary on K-pop, K-dramas, C-dramas, and pop culture. We read the stories the world is telling — and find God hidden in plain sight inside them.

About the founder

A love letter
to culture

Flip The Script began as a simple conviction: that God is not absent from the stories the world is telling. He is hidden in plain sight — in every drama that wrecks you, every song that names what you couldn't, every character you fall in love with and can't explain why.

"When you're in love with Jesus, you see Him everywhere. Every moment becomes a conversation with the Holy Spirit."

I write Christ-centered reflections at the intersection of faith and everyday life — and I'm rallying a community of believers to pray for the artists and fans that Asian pop culture is shaping. Because the heart of every K-pop fan or drama lover is a heart that was made for God.
